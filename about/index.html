Who We Are

Pegasus Investigations and VIP Protection Security Firm is a licensed security firm under the Ministry of Home Affairs and Cultural Heritage in Zimbabwe (REFERENCE NUMBER/B9a/1/3108). We provide professional investigative, criminal defense investigations, intelligence-support and executive protection services to clients who require discretion, accuracy and lawful execution in high-consequence matters. Our work supports informed decision-making in criminal defense, risk management and personal security where outcomes depend on facts, timing and professional judgment.

The firm was established to meet the demand for independent, disciplined and ethically grounded investigative and protective services in an increasingly complex legal and security environment. Pegasus Investigations serves clients who value clarity over speculation, substance over appearance and professionalism over publicity. Our investigative services are evidence-driven and methodical, designed to support criminal defense strategy, verify material facts, reconstruct events and identify gaps in existing inquiries. We approach each assignment with analytical rigor, maintaining strict confidentiality and adherence to legal and evidentiary standards applicable to judicial proceedings.

Our executive and VIP protection services are intelligence-led, preventative and deliberately low-profile. Emphasis is placed on advance planning threat and risk assessment, situational awareness and movement security with the objective of preventing incidents rather than responding to them. Pegasus Investigations operates under clear principles of legality, discretion, professionalism and accountability. All services are conducted within the boundaries of applicable law and ethical practice. We do not engage in unlawful surveillance, coercive methods or activities that compromise human rights, judicial integrity or client credibility.

Our clientele includes legal practitioners, government, private individuals, corporate entities and high-risk or high-profile persons requiring reliable investigative insight or protective support. Each engagement is evaluated individually to ensure lawful scope, ethical clarity and operational suitability. Pegasus Investigations and VIP Protection Security Firm remains committed to delivering precise, confidential and professionally executed services where the margin for error is minimal and discretion is essential.
