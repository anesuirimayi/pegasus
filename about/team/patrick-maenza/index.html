Professional Profile

Biography

Mr. Patrick Maenza is an accredited and certified criminal investigator, intelligence analyst and private investigator with expertise in counter-terrorism, close protection and close-quarter battle operations.

He holds a Shotokan Yellow Belt, is an electrical engineer and a certified media and journalism practitioner.

Currently advancing his studies in forensic science, criminal intelligence and modern investigative techniques, Mr. Maenza combines field experience with an entrepreneurial mindset and strong patriotic commitment. As Managing Director, he oversees all criminal defense investigation cases, working closely with the Director of Investigations and Undercover Operations to ensure meticulous, strategic, and results-driven outcomes.
