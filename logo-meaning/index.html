Complete Logo Interpretation

The upright, forward-facing sword at the center of the logo stands as a timeless emblem of justice, authority and defense. The firm's unwavering commitment to uphold justice, law and national security whether through lethal intervention when absolutely necessary or through diplomatic precision rooted in lawful procedure.

The presence of blood splatter across the sword serves twofold in its meaning. First, it denotes the scientific rigor of forensic investigation, representing blood pattern analysis and DNA recovery - the foundation of modern criminal resolution. Reflecting our pledge to investigate with integrity, evidence, and scientific truth.

Second, it solemnly honors the sacrificial blood shed by comrades during the Chimurenga - Umvukela struggles, whose heroic acts laid the foundation of Zimbabwe's sovereignty. Their courage is etched into the DNA of our operations, reminding us that our security mandate is built upon their blood-stained legacy.

The flag embedded or waved behind the sword affirms our firm's absolute loyalty to Zimbabwe's Constitution, sovereignty and democratic leadership. It conveys unquestionable patriotism, placing national interest and security above all and binding the institution to lawful governance and institutional ethics. It also represents unity, identity, and duty - values that every operative within Pegasus must live by.

The lightning embedded in the logo is a symbol of speed, accuracy and tactical precision. It reflects our ability to respond rapidly under pressure, execute with surgical effectiveness and make decisive interventions within critical timeframes. In every operation, Pegasus meets nothing less than Tier 01 standards, the benchmark of elite excellence in private security and investigations.

The bolt reminds our operatives that every second counts and in that second our action must be swift, calculated and flawless.
