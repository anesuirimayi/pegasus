CRIMINAL DEFENSE INVESTIGATIONS

Supporting legal strategy with disciplined, evidence-focused inquiry

Pegasus Investigations and VIP Protection Security Firm provides independent criminal defense investigative services designed to support legal counsel and private clients in serious and complex criminal matters. We conduct methodical, evidence-focused inquiries that complement legal strategy while respecting judicial procedures and the rule of law. Our work is carried out with strict confidentiality and in accordance with applicable investigative and evidentiary standards.

Scope of Services

Our criminal defense investigations may include:

  • Independent case review and reconstruction
  • Timeline and event-sequence analysis
  • Verification and assessment of prosecution evidence
  • Identification of inconsistencies, gaps, or omissions in existing investigations
  • Witness identification, location, and professional interviewing
  • Background and credibility assessments
  • Scene and location analysis (where lawful and accessible)
  • Collection and preservation of defense-relevant information
  • Preparation of structured investigative reports for legal review

Our Approach

Each assignment begins with a comprehensive review of available case materials and legal objectives. Investigations are conducted with analytical rigor, impartiality, and respect for evidentiary integrity. We focus on facts, documentation, and verifiable information, avoiding speculation or conjecture.

Where appropriate, our findings are presented in clear, structured formats suitable for consultation with legal practitioners and, where permitted, for consideration within judicial proceedings.

Professional & Legal Standards

Pegasus Investigations operates independently and does not interfere with law enforcement processes or judicial authority. We do not engage in unlawful surveillance, evidence manipulation, witness intimidation, or obstruction of justice.

All investigative activities are conducted lawfully, ethically, and with full respect for human rights and due process.

Who This Service Is For

  • Legal practitioners and defense attorneys
  • Individuals facing criminal allegations
  • Families seeking clarity in complex cases
  • Clients requiring an independent review of investigative facts

Confidentiality: All criminal defense investigations are handled with strict confidentiality. Information is disclosed only to authorized parties as agreed contractually and in accordance with the law.
